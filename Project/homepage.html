About the Program

Sheng's MBA program gives you the opportunity to explore career options while developing your business acumen through real-world experience. Using our relationships with some of Canada’s best employers, we developed a new curriculum. It gives you the modern business skills you need along with the opportunity to apply them during three paid work terms. If you graduated from a North American university, you don’t need work experience to apply. You get the experience you need while completing your degree.

What you need to know about Sheng's MBA program

Specializations

Sheng’s MBA offers specializations in the areas of highest demand. Choose from Consulting, Finance, Healthcare Management, Marketing & Sales or Entrepreneurship & Innovation.

Team-Based Learning

Sheng's MBA Program has been a pioneer in the important area of team-based learning and utilizes an approach that is far more sophisticated than that used in other MBA programs.

Double Degrees

In today’s competitive environment, there are some career paths that demand a level of specialization beyond the MBA program. Double Degree options are designed to accommodate these demands.

International Exchange

If international exposure is integral to your career goals, Sheng’s MBA offers exchange opportunities at more than 100 of the world’s top international business schools.

Applied Consulting Project

The applied business research consulting project provides you, our MBA candidates, with the unique opportunity to provide consulting services to a live client.

CPA Designation

Sheng's MBA program offers a CPA-Accredited stream that provides graduates with advanced standing in the CPA Professional Education Program (CPA PEP) to the end of Core 2.
